About Orderly Spaces

Hi, I'm so glad
you're here.

I have loved organizing for as long as I can remember. There's something so satisfying about watching a cluttered, overwhelming space transform into something functional and calm — and I've seen firsthand the difference it can make in someone's daily life.

My mission is simple: to help you take the spaces that feel dysfunctional and stressful, and turn them into places that bring you peace. You deserve to walk through your front door and take a deep breath — not brace yourself. That's what I'm here for.

I don't believe in complicated systems or Pinterest-perfect solutions that fall apart after a week. My approach is practical, simple, and built around your real life — so things actually stay organized even on your busiest days. I meet you exactly where you are, with zero judgment and a whole lot of encouragement.

— Your organizing partner
What I offer

Services designed for real life

🏡

Home Organizing

Whether it's a kitchen that's become a catch-all, closets that no longer close, a playroom that's taken over, or a garage you've given up on — I can tackle any room in the house. We'll start by sorting and decluttering together, then I'll take it from there — building out the systems while you go about your day. When it's all done, you get to come back to the big reveal. (Want to stay and be part of the whole process? You're more than welcome — it's entirely up to you.)

In-home sessions
💻

Virtual Organizing

Not local, or just prefer to tackle things on your own terms? Virtual organizing is a great option — and a perfect fit if you're a hands-on, DIY type who just needs some direction and accountability to get started. Over video call, I'll guide you through the process step by step, offer input as you work through decisions, and help keep you on track. It's flexible, affordable, and available no matter where you live. Distance is no barrier to an organized home.

Remote & flexible
📅

Maintenance Sessions

Getting organized is one thing — staying that way is another. Life happens: kids, work, seasons change, and even the best systems need a little reset now and then. Maintenance sessions are available exclusively to existing Orderly Spaces clients, and can be scheduled seasonally, twice a year, or annually — whatever works best for you. We'll refresh your space, troubleshoot anything that's shifted, and make any adjustments if your current systems aren't quite fitting your life anymore. Think of it as a tune-up for your home.

For existing clients
✨

Deep Cleaning

Sometimes a space just needs a truly fresh start. I offer thorough deep cleaning services — the kind that goes beyond the surface and gets into all the spots that regular cleaning misses. From baseboards to forgotten corners, I'll give your home the reset it deserves. I use non-toxic, effective cleaning products that are powerful without the harsh chemical smells. Please note this service does not include shampooing carpets or upholstery.
