About Orderly Spaces

Hi, I'm so glad
you're here.

I started Orderly Spaces because I genuinely believe that the state of your home has a direct impact on your peace of mind — and I've seen firsthand what a difference an organized space can make in someone's life.

My approach isn't about perfection or Pinterest-worthy aesthetics. It's about creating systems that actually work for you, your family, and your lifestyle. I meet you exactly where you are — no judgment, just practical, compassionate help.

Whether you're drowning in clutter, moving into a new home, or just need help maintaining the order you've already built, I'm here to make it feel manageable — and even enjoyable.

What I offer

Services designed for real life

🏡

Home Organizing

Maybe your kitchen has become a catch-all, your closets no longer close, or you just can't figure out why nothing ever feels in its right place. I'll come to your home and work alongside you to sort, declutter, and create functional systems that are easy to maintain. We'll tackle your space at a pace that feels comfortable — because the goal isn't just to organize it once, it's to set you up for the long run. You deserve a home that feels like a breath of fresh air every time you walk in.

In-home sessions
💻

Virtual Organizing

Not in the area, or prefer to work from the comfort of your own home? Virtual organizing is a surprisingly powerful option. Over video call, I'll guide you through the process step by step — helping you make decisions, create systems, and stay accountable — all without anyone needing to set foot through your door. It's flexible, affordable, and incredibly effective for people who just need a little direction and encouragement to get started. Distance is no barrier to an organized home.

Remote & flexible
📅

Maintenance Sessions

Getting organized is one thing — staying that way is another. Life happens: kids, work, seasons change, and even the best systems need a little reset. Maintenance sessions are shorter, focused check-ins designed to help you refresh your space and troubleshoot anything that's slipped. Think of it as a tune-up for your home. These sessions are perfect for existing clients who want ongoing support, or anyone who just needs a little help keeping the momentum going between bigger projects.

Ongoing support
✨

Deep Cleaning

Sometimes a space needs a fresh, clean slate before organizing can truly shine. As I'm growing my client base, I'm offering deep cleaning services to complement the organizing process — because it's so much easier to build new systems in a space that feels truly clean and reset. From baseboards to forgotten corners, I'll give your home the thorough clean it deserves. This service works beautifully alongside an organizing session, setting the perfect foundation for the transformation ahead.

Limited availability
🌿
A note on Deep Cleaning: This service is currently offered on a limited, temporary basis while I'm building my organizing client base. It pairs wonderfully with any organizing session — reach out to discuss availability and bundling options.
